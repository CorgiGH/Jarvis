Secondarily "PM looked at every frame" is unenforceable honor-system; a 30-frame thumbnail grid invites the same partial-attention skim. KEY CONCERN: the mutation assertion runs on state, not pixels — it would have GREEN-LIT the inert frames; the gate must diff rendered output.

## ⚙️ Pragmatist

## 🧱 First Principles
