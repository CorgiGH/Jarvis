## 1. Summary verdict

### (a) The 3 template demo lessons — structurally sound, content-correctness failures concentrated in QR

The lesson **mechanics pass every structural gate in all 12 theme×viewport combos**: zero element clipping, zero horizontal overflow, zero interactive-element overlap, zero ellipsis truncation. Every gate genuinely locks (`continua` disabled + non-advancing until predict/attempt/trace/seen/check satisfied, gate message shown); wrong-answer and correct-answer feedback paths both render; predict-callback personalization works in all 3 lessons; zero JS console errors (one benign favicon 404). **MergeSort is the gold standard**: learner-paced scrubber (back/play/forward/restart + `pas N/9` counter), camera re-fits per step, callout always live.

What fails is **what the figures teach and whether the learner can drive them**:
- **QR beat-1 actively contradicts its own lesson** — the reflection animation visibly stretches the vector 2.39× while the legend and payoff line state length preservation ("aceeasi lungime"), and after the animation the x/Hx labels collide, the vector lands on the unlabeled negative half of e1, and the FIX#2 guide line is degenerate (never renders).
- **The lesson's payoff (the zeroed cells) is nearly invisible** at ~1.75:1 contrast in QR beats 2 and 4, both themes.
- **Dijkstra beat-3 violates the locked learner-paced template**: one-shot ~17s run, no pause/scrub/step, and the play button is destroyed on completion — replay is impossible, revisits show a dead figure.
- Plus an EN leak ("skeleton"/"skeletonul") in QR beat-3, the confirmed double-"2." numbering, the beat-2 ~194px top-gap layout jump, theme-broken color naming ("galben" labeling a coral arrow), and top-pooled composition in MergeSort/Dijkstra at 1440×900.

All 4 previously-known pending bugs were **confirmed with measured evidence**; 5 new findings were added (zero-cell contrast, EN leak, warm-theme color mismatch, top-pooling, faint gata-button).

### (b) The in-app viz gallery — mechanically solid, systemically wrong language

The **machinery is clean**: zero console errors/pageerrors, zero 4xx/5xx, no page-level horizontal overflow, no interactive-element overlaps, all 16 AlgoStepperShell steppers advance correctly (frame counter verified 1→4), the MatrixTransform prediction gate blocks/unblocks correctly, CompareFrames' ANIMATE cycles modes. Programmatic "overflow" hits were all intentional visually-hidden aria-live regions at `left:-9999`.

The dominant violation is **language: 100% of learner-facing copy in all 20 components is English** — frame captions/narration, diagram labels, full legend sentences, the PREDICT gate question + answer chips, stepper chrome (FRAME/RESET/SHARE/VOICE OFF/PREDICT), and aria-live narration. This is a hard-line gate breach (lesson content must be Romanian) across the entire component family, with two fix loci (shell-level chrome vs per-component captions) but blanket scope. Rendered-quality failures on top: SigmaStackedBar's label pile-up is genuinely illegible (and its all-black segments confirm the known suspect), TLS 0-RTT truncates the pedagogically load-bearing "Finished" message, and a band of medium overlap/truncation/missing-axis issues spans SchedulerGantt, OsiEncap, NPGadget, CppVTable, and the PS-basis primitives.
